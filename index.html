How to play

Lets Play!

Its YOU vs The Computer!
Roll the dice 3 times, whoever has the highest score after 3 rounds is declared the winner!
However, Roll a 1 and no points are scored that round.
Roll a pair and the score is doubled for that round!
To roll the dice, click 'Roll Dice'. Start a new game at any time by hitting 'New Game'
